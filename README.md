# UFO Exploration
_How can I maximize my chances of seeing a UFO?_

## Motivation and Data Question

UFOs have remained in popular interest for decades. Although now most don’t anticipate a "The War of the Worlds," hundreds of sightings are reported to the National UFO Reporting Center each month. I explore the timing, locations, and shapes of the UFOs reported. I compare them to locations of military bases as listed by the US DOT. I am looking for patterns that may reveal where I can find a UFO, too.
